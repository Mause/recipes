## Salad Dressing

## History

This recipe was originally stolen from the Swiss restauraunt where my grandma worked :)

### Ingredients

 * Salt or aromat
 * pepper
 * curry
 * dill
 * maggi or soyasauce
 * mustard
 * mayo
 * some vinegar
 * double the amount of oil than you had vinegar

## Method

1. Mix all and adjust to taste
